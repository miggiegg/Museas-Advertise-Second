Let’s face it – most users hate ads, and rightfully so. Advertisements are becoming more pervasive, obnoxious, and intrusive. On computers and smartphones, adblockers are increasingly being installed to bypass ads. The truth is, we hate bad ads too.  As a solution to this challenge, Museas has developed a strategy that aims to undue this negative perception. Our approach is to collaborate with sponsors to create advertisements that users will enjoy, without feeling tracked or targeted. 
In addition to advertisements, sponsors can promote and sell their admission tickets, event tickets, local merchandise, and more right through the Museas Store. On the website or apps, it is is a convenient, fast, and secure place to shop.  And with full support for Apple Pay®, patrons can pay with TouchID or FaceID for a quicker, easier, and more secure transaction.
Pricing and Terms
With a unique, engaged, and effective user base, advertisements on Museas deliver real, measurable business results. Museas offers competitive pricing options personalized for the size and requirements of your business. Review the latest Pricing Sheet or contact us for more information.

Museas is a platform where museums and sponsors alike can be successful in promoting its business with the same level of engagement and professionalism our audience expects. As a rule of thumb, advertisements must be related to art, culture, food, entertainment, or similar subject matter relevant to the Museas platform.
Tickets  Promote museum admission tickets on the front page of the Museas Store. Patrons can purchase tickets online to skip the lines and spend more time inside. Museums and customers receive an instant email confirming ticket purchase, and each transaction is protected with a unique ID to deter scams. Events  Local occasions such as festivals, markets, concerts, or theatre are demonstrated interests for users of Museas. Further market these events through advertisements or online ticketing sales on Museas. Upon purchase, patrons receive a digital pass on their mobile devices for a quick and convenient admission. Merchandise  Increase product sales and market recognition. Local merchandise such as gifts, souvenirs, and artisan works can be promoted and sold on the Museas Store. In addition, Museas offers logistics and inventory services to deliver product to U.S. customers.
